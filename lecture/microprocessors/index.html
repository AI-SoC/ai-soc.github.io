Microprocessor Lecture Introduction

INTRODUCTION

The every smart systems and internet connection of things (IoT) are based on
microcontrollers/microprocessors, which are powered by specially-designed
firmware and dedicated hardware. In this course, you will study MCU-based
approach to program everything around us, especially about:

  • learn both hardware and software aspects of integrating digital devices
    (such as memory and I/O interfaces) into microprocessor-based systems
  • get practical experience in applied digital systems design based on
    ARM (cortex-m) assembly-language programming
