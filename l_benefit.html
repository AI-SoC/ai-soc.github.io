Support and Benefit for Members

Financial Support

Top Class Financial Support in Korea (60,000,000 Won per year per student)

  • Ph.D Students get 3,000,000 Won x 12 months = 36,000,000 Won (No Tax) + 24,000,000 Won Research Activity Support

  • 60,000,000 Won is fully allocated for individual student, for stable research concentration

Academia-Industry Collabration Internship Program

All students have various chances participating active internship program with collaboration with Researchers in the followings:

  • National Research Centers (ETRI, ADD, KETI)

  • Industry Company (Samsung, LG, Hyundai)

Visiting Scholar

All students have various chances coworking with international researchers in USA as a visiting researcher during Ph.D course

International Conference Participation

Students with the accepted papers can get support for entire travel cost including boarding pass ticket, minimum twice per year (Budget 12,000,000 Won per year per student).

  • All students travel every year for 4 times international conference participation such as France, England, USA, Japan, Taiwan, Singapore

  • Internship students (B.S) also have useful chance to present their works in international conferences.

Journal Paper Publication

All students submit 4 papers into KCI or SCI-indexed journal per year. (Budget 12,000,000 Won per year per student)

  • Ph.D students have average 5 SCI journal papers and 10 conference papers until graduation.

  • M.S students also have average minimum one SCI journal paper and 5 conference papers until graduation.

Technical Experience

Special Expert Training Courses to be Ace of Field Engineers

  • Various Industrial Project for Embedded Software Development (Supported by Samsung, LG, Hyundai)

  • System-on-Chip VLSI Circuit Design and Silicon IC Implementation (Supported by Samsung, Hanhwa, LG, Hyundai, IDEC)

  • Expensive Software Tools such as Cross-Compiler, Simulator, VLSI Chip Design Tools such as Cadence, Synopsys, Mentor

Computing Environment

Private PC with triple LCD monitors, high performance laptop (notebook) are given to all students. A partitioned space filled with the fresh air by standalone air-purifier is allocated for independent research. All equipments and materials including FPGA systems for experiment are given with the unlimited cost.

 

Facilities

Our Lab provides multi-processors Zeon-powered CAD workstation for SoC circuit design, DELL's 2 Zeon CPU-based high performance computing platform with 4 GPUs (NVidia 1080TI) for large-scaled simulation acceleration and the computing platform equipped with Alveo FPGA accelerator. Students can design their SoC using full licenses of Synopsys, Cadence, Mentor software. They can enjoy their rest using coffee machine, ice maker, and toaster.
